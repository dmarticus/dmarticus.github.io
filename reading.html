Reading

I love to read and while I don't set any yearly or monthly goals on how many books I read or anything like that (I don't like raw numbers as a means of quantifying the value one receives from literature), writing my reading list down in a discoverable place has come in handy when recommending books to family or friends. Right now it's just books, but I may add a some additional sections to encompass of other forms of reading (such as longform or shortform articles).
